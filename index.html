TAIWAN!

I know, you’re probably thinking, “Why should I let my child go halfway across the globe for a fun vacation?” First of all, I’m hardly a child anymore (almost 15!). Also, according to Google, the most trusted source in the world: Taiwan is a QUARTER of the distance around the globe from Canada. The only thing you got right was the fun vacation part, but even that’s an understatement. Taiwan is THE BEST vacation destination, plus, I’ve already thought of every single positive of going:

  1. IT’S CHEAP! (Isn’t that the first thing you look for?)

    Despite being a quarter of the way across the globe, it’s fairly cheap to fly to Taiwan, only $851 for a round-trip. And if that’s not enough, I’ve got all the details about the prices also on this webpage!

  2. Shelter

    I know you’re extremely concerned about where I would live during the 2-month vacation, so I’ve also got that covered! For the most part, I’ll be living in our house in Taiwan, and for the rest, I’ll live in cheap hotels.

  3. DELICIOUS (and slightly healthy) FOOD!

    Taiwan’s food and drinks are THE BEST, with a wide range of food, like bubble tea and pork intestine noodles, which sounds nasty but is amazing (I speak from personal experience). Additionally, I could always go for a homemade meal every once in a while to maintain health.

  4. E N J O Y A B L E

    Of course, this is the least of your priorities, but I couldn’t go on without talking about all of the attractions Taiwan has to offer: epic views, amusement parks, TRAMPOLINE PARKS, and your personal favourite, museums! On top of that, Taiwan is close to neighbouring countries like Korea, and I can take guided trips there for a cheaper price!
